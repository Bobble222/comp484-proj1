Dogs are great and amazing in many ways, and deserve the moniker they have obtained in the past as man's best friend. Here are a few reasons why you should have one!

Floofiness

From the most puffiest of puffballs to just skin and bones, there's a dog with an ideal floof ratio to your liking!

Attention

Dogs can act like attention junkies craving your every moment, or more aloof like cats tend to be.

Responsibility

Getting a dog is a heavy task for a person, as it comes with many responsibilities, similar to raising a child, but far easier.
